About Phoenix Plants Lifesaver

Your trusted partner in agricultural excellence since 2019

Phoenix Plants Lifesaver Pvt. Ltd. started our journey in 2019 with a vision to make a transforming impact in agriculture society. We observed the problems and issues of basic Agriculture System.

We address challenge of farming in typical rural areas and start working to finding the solutions of these issues from grass-roots level. At first, we marked the challenges of farmers which are stopping them to uplift their farming culture. After this we address the problems of whole retail channel which are forcing them to stuck in traditional business practices, which are totally outdated and have lower financial statics. Then we acknowledge the network across the country and found that we are in a huge trouble in terms of productions of core fertilizers and distribution statices across the country.

We collected all these facts and issues by working with farmer, retailer and traders’ communities of multiple geographical areas. After all of this observation we stabilised the organisation Phoenix Plants Lifesaver Pvt. Ltd. with a vision to make a transforming and developing impact in this Agriculture Society.

Shipping & Return Policy

Clear delivery and return guidelines to help distributors and customers order with confidence.

Shipping Policy

Fast and reliable dispatch

Orders are usually processed within 1 to 3 working days after payment or order confirmation. Dispatch timelines can vary during peak agricultural seasons, public holidays, or for bulk distributor orders.

  • Delivery timelines depend on product availability and delivery location.
  • Customers will be contacted if there is any dispatch delay for special or large-volume orders.
  • Please share a correct delivery address and contact number to avoid shipment issues.
  • Visible package damage should be reported at the time of delivery or immediately after receipt.
Return Policy

Support for damaged or incorrect items

Returns are accepted only for products that arrive damaged, defective, expired, or incorrect. Return or replacement requests should be raised within 48 hours of delivery with order details and product photos.

  • Opened, used, or improperly stored products may not be eligible for return.
  • Approved return cases may be resolved through replacement, credit note, or refund after verification.
  • Refund timelines depend on the payment mode and approval process.
  • For help, customers can connect through the contact information listed on this page.
